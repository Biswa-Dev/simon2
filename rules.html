Simon Rules-

  1. Press the START button. Simon will give the first signal. Press the first signal.
  2. In next level Simon will give another signal. You have to press the previous level signal and then current level signal in order.
  3. In each new level simon will duplicate the previous level signals in sequence and add a new signal.
  4. Rememveber the sequence of signals and press them in order in each new level.
  5. Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
